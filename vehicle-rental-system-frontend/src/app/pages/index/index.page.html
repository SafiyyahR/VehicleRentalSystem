FAQs

Where will the pickup and drop off location be?

At BIA (Bandaranayake International Airport), Colombo, Sri Lanka.

 What is the age required to rent a vehicle?

The age depends on the vehicle chosen to rent. For example:
Bicycle: 18
Motorbike: 25
Any other vehicle: 20

Will any additional fees be charged after returning the vehicle?

None, unless the vehicle is damaged when returned.

 Are any documents required to be shown before picking up the vehicle?

Yes. The following documents are required:

  1. Driving License
  2. Proof of identity(Identity Card or Passport)
  3. A valid debit or credit card.
